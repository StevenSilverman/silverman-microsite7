JUMP INTO IT

BMX is an extreme sport that has had a large uprising over the past couple decades. Taking inspiration from skate culture, BMX culture has made their own sub-group of extreme sports that is often compared to skateboarding. Here at MassBMX, our goal is to bring the best of the sport to light. We feature the hottest skateparks in the Massachusetts area, as well as places you can stay a while. Professional riders from all around the area rely on MassBMX for their updates. Sign up for the ride of your life.

RIDE HERE

Choose from our curated list of skateparks around the Massachusetts area. These parks are ridden by the best of the Massachusetts riders and recommended to any rider.

Pittsfield Park

Pittsfield Skatepark

A small park, featuring bowls and a vert wall for vert riders, rails and ledges for technical riders, and stairs and gaps for hardcore riders.

North Adams Park

North Adams Skatepark

Larger park with a variety of quarter pipes, rails, kickers, and ledges. Perfect for any type of rider, since street spots surround the park.

Northampton Park

Northampton Skatepark

Another small park more built for vert riders who want to practice air tricks more than technical tricks such as rails.

Rad Indoor Park

Rad Skatepark

A popular wooden indoor park across Massachusetts. Fit for many riders, with fly-outs, as well as technical sections with ledges and rails.

HEAR HERE

Massachusetts produces some of the most hardcore, dedicated riders. With riders sponsored with large companies such as Sunday, OSS, and even Vans, Massachusetts knows BMX. These riders turn to MassBMX in order to keep up to date with the culture, without losing out on precious riding time.
